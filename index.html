Privacy Policy — Quak Fit

Last updated: 2025-12-03

1. Overview

Quak Fit is designed to work completely offline. We do not collect, store, process, or share any personal information.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

The app does not collect or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal data (such as name, email address, phone number, or identifiers)
  • Usage data or analytics
  • Location data
  • Contacts, photos, or files
  • Device information or device identifiers
  • Cookies or tracking technologies

3. Device Permissions

The app does not request or require any device permissions.

4. Offline Functionality

All features operate without an internet connection. No data ever leaves your device.

5. Third-Party Services

The app:

  • Does not display ads
  • Does not use analytics tools
  • Does not integrate any third-party SDKs
  • Does not share data with any external services

6. Children’s Privacy

Since the app does not collect any personal information, it is suitable for users of all ages.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we introduce features that require data collection in the future, we will update this Privacy Policy and clearly inform users within the app or through an updated policy.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at: pato@quak.fit.
