Privacy Policy — Quak Fit

Last updated: 2025-12-05

1. Overview

Quak Fit is a fitness and nutrition app that provides training routines, exercise guides, and healthy recipes. The app allows users to create an account to access personalized content. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the rights you have regarding your data.

2. Information We Collect

When you create an account or use the app, we may collect and process the following information:

2.1 Personal Information

  • Email address
  • Password (securely stored and never visible to us)
  • Internal user ID

2.2 App Usage Information

We store certain data to provide the core functionality of the app, such as:

  • Completed workouts or activities
  • Selected training plans
  • Saved recipes or favorites

Quak Fit does not collect:

  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Photos or media files
  • Device identifiers for advertising purposes
  • Advertising or marketing tracking data

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information strictly to provide and improve the core features of the app, including:

  • Creating and managing your user account
  • Allowing access to personalized content (plans, recipes, workouts)
  • Saving your activity history
  • Providing account support and enabling account deletion

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage and Security

All account and app data is stored securely using Supabase (PostgreSQL and authentication services). Supabase implements industry-standard encryption and security practices.

Passwords are hashed and never accessible to Quak Fit. We take reasonable measures to protect your data, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Account Deletion

You may delete your account at any time directly from within the app. Deleting your account permanently removes:

  • Your authentication record
  • Your user profile
  • Your activity history
  • Associated personalized data stored for your account

Account deletion cannot be undone. Once your account is deleted, you will no longer be able to access your previous data.

6. Third-Party Services

6.1 Supabase

We use Supabase to store account data and deliver app functionality. Supabase acts as a data processor on our behalf and does not use your information for independent purposes such as advertising.

6.2 Open Food Facts (Data Source Only)

Nutritional data in the app comes from the collaborative database Open Food Facts. We do not send any personal data to Open Food Facts.

Data is reused under the Open Database License (ODbL).

7. Children’s Privacy

Quak Fit is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data (available directly within the app)

If you have questions about your data or wish to exercise any of these rights, you can contact us using the details below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. When we make significant updates, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify users within the app.

10. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, you can contact us at: pato@quak.fit.
